Uprooted offers seasonal and themed menus that are entirely vegan and gluten-free. Dishes are vegetable-forward, often incorporating a playful reinvention of classic favorites. Our hope is that everyone who dines with us leaves feeling not only full but utterly delighted.

Currently, uprooted dinners are hosted as pop-up events. Follow us on Instagram for sneak peeks and updates, and be sure to check our pop-up page for information about upcoming menus and dates.
